makes 1 serving

this follows the idea of [[seed sync/seed cycling]] and [[cycle syncing]]
### ingredients
- 40g rolled oats
- 10g hemp hearts
- 10g chia seeds
- 10g flax seeds
- 10g pumpkin seeds
- 170ml plant milk
- pinch of cinnamon 
- pinch of sea salt 
- sweetener (optional)
